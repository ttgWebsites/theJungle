Characters

  • Jurgis Rudkus
  • Jurgis is a Lithuanian American immigrant
  • He moves to America with his family to start a new life and live the American dream
  • He lives in Chicago and works in Packingtown in a meat packing plant early on, however his life will soon become terribly difficult
  • Jurgis faces the struggles of many Americans during the early 1900's as he can barely afford to feed his family and must go from job to job trying to find any work he can
  • Ona
  • Jurgis' wife, Ona moves to America with the same dream as Jurgis
  • The couple is happy at first, however soon realizes the mistake of their choice as life is very difficult
  • She lives a very rough life in Chicago before her death during child birth, at one point she is even raped by her boss
  • Marija Berczynskas
  • She is Ona's cousin and travels to America with Ona and Jurgis in order to seek a new life
  • She however suffers in Chicago as well despite initially proving to be strong a persevering
  • Eventually she must take up prostitution to support her children
  • Jonas
  • Teta's brother, he is the inspiration behind the move to America
  • Shortly after moving to Chicago he disappears and the family suffers from the loss of wages he brings in
  • Teta Elzbieta Lukoszaite
  • Ona's stepmother, as well as the mother of another six children
  • She is a strong woman and despite the hardships she faces she fights on to support her family
  • She is a very important character to the novel as she represents family and tradition
  • Phil Connor
  • He is the boss of Ona early on in the novel
  • Phil takes advantage of Ona and forces her to sleep with him by raping her and threatening to fire her and her family members if she exposes him
  • He represents the corruptness in the Chicago meat packing industry as he can destroy the families life with a few simple steps
  • Dede Antanas Rudkus
  • Jonas' father, he travels to America with the family, yet this turns out a bad decision
  • He has difficulty finding work because of his age, and eventually dies because of poor health as a result from the few jobs he works which expose him to unsanitary conditions
  • Antanas Rudkus
  • Ona and Jurgis' young son, he is the reason Jurgis fights to live and support the family
  • One day after Ona has already died, Antanas dies in a freak accident which crushes Jurgis as he is the only thing driving Jurgis to live
  • Stanislovas Lukoszaita
  • One of Teta's children, he must work at a young age to help support the family
  • He eventually dies in an accident at work when he falls asleep in a bad area
  • Jack Duane
  • A criminal Jurgis meets on a few occasions in prison
  • Jack convinces Jurgis to enter a life of crime in order to support himself after Jurgis has been to jail a few times
  • Mike Skully
  • A corrupt businessman in Chicago, he meets Jurgis through the criminal underground
  • He offers Jurgis an opportunity to work as a scab, as well as work to convince people to vote a certain way in exchange for three hundred dollars
  • Tommy Hinds
  • He is the owner of a hotel and gives Jurgis a job near the end of the novel
  • Hinds is a socialist, along with Jurgis, and the two work to spread the party
  • Ostrinski
  • A Polish immigrant who convinces Jurgis to join the socialist party
