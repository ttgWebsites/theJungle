Chapter Summaries

  • Chapter 1
  • The novel begins in the early 1900's as a Lithuanian couple Ona Lukoszaite and Jurgis Rudkus celebrate a feast in honor of their upcoming wedding
  • The couple has just moved to Chicago and celebrates the feast near a stockyard in the center of the cities meat packing district
  • The feast is a wonderful and joyous occasion filled with great food, loud music, and customary Lithuanian dancing
  • Being that the feast is very expensive it is tradition that during the dances men will put money in hat held by Ona's stepmother, Teta, in order to help pay for the feast
  • The bill for the feast approaches 300 dollars, a very significant sum at the time, and many of the guests choose not to offer any donations leaving the young couple to pay for most of it themselves
  • The reader learns that most of the people attending the festivities work in the low-paying meat packing business
  • They must work terrible hours, and cannot be late even a minute without losing pay
  • Chapter 2
  • Chapter two goes into detail about Jurgis and his family
  • Jurgis and his soon to be wife Ona lived a poor lifestyle back in Lithuania as Ona's father had died leaving behind a large debt
  • The couple had heard of other Lithuanian's who had traveled to America and made a much better living
  • The two decide that they should make a journey and start a new life, yet Ona does not want to leave behind her family and vows to bring them with
  • Jurgis spends months working to save money for the journey, meanwhile the list of who is traveling with them grows as well as Ona's cousin, Marija, joins the group as well as Jurgis' father, Dede Antanas
  • Due to the large group and people ripping Jurgis off over the course of the journey, the family is nearly broke when it arrives in Chicago
  • While in Chicago Jurgis runs into a Lithuanian man who owns a delicatessen
  • Jurgis asks for help and advice, the man, Jokubas Szedvilas, gives him the address of a boardinghouse where the family moves to
  • After moving in to their new home Jurgis and Ona explore the streets of Packingtown and witness the horrible sites and stenches of the meat packing industry
  • Chapter 3
  • Jokubas offers to take the whole family on a tour of Packingtown
  • Everyone is shocked by the conditions of the animals that will be soon turned into meat
  • They are smashed together in tight pens and in very unsanitary conditions
  • Jokubas mentions that the people who check the sanitary conditions often tends to overlook many animals and that the packing industry often prepares contaminated meat for sale
  • Chapter 4
  • Jurgis finally finds a job sweeping up entrails of cattle at one of the meat packing plants
  • Though the job is not the most ideal, Jurgis is optimistic as he earns over two dollars a day, and its a starting place
  • Marija has also found a job, and Jonas seems to be close to finding a job
  • Jurgis however will not allow Ona, Teta Elzbieta, or his children to work, as he wants his children to attend school
  • His oldest son Stanislovas would be eligible to work, however Jurgis insists on school for him as well
  • Dede, Jurgis father, does not have as much luck with finding a job as he is too old to compete for work
  • One day the family discovers an add for new homes
  • It seems too good to be true as the add states that they can purchase a new four-bedroom home for 300 dollars down and 12 dollars a month
  • Ona, Marija, and Teta visit the real estate agent one day while Jurgis is at work and begin to work out the details of the payment
  • Though the house is not as described, and suspicions of a scam arise, they eventually agree on the deal
  • Jurgis returns from work that evening and learns what has happened and is outraged as he believes that his family is being scammed
  • He notices that the home is listed as a rental, and believes that eventually they will lose the money and the home
  • He sets out to make it right and learns that it is referred to as a rental so that if a family fails to make payments they can be evicted
  • This eases Jurgis' min
  • Chapter 5
  • The family prepares for the move and eventually moves in
  • The new home is very nice for the family and Jurgis is very happy with both it and his job
  • He learns from co-workers that pretty much everyone at the meat packing plant hates their job
  • Jurgis doesn't understand why, as he actually enjoys his job. He refuses to join the workers union fighting for a slower pace of work
  • One day Dede is offered a job, however he must pay one third of his wages to the man offering it
  • Jurgis is surprised by this, but he learns that this is very common, and nearly everyone must be corrupt to some extent in order to survive
  • Dede agrees to take the job, and it turns out that he is working packing disgusting meat that humans will eat
  • Meanwhile Marija comes to realize that her job was originally held by a fifteen year old worker, and Jonas' job was previously held by someone who died as a result of the conditions of work
  • Jurgis begins to notice that in his work, calf fetuses and diseased meat are mixed in with other meat for human consumption
  • Chapter 6
  • A nearby neighbor to the family, Grandmother Majauszkiene, tells the family that the house they bought is a scam
  • She explains that nearly everyone cannot afford the payments as they must pay interest, and that even one months missed rent means they will be evicted and lose everything already paid
  • She adds that the houses were made out of cheap material and are much older than advertised
  • The family also learns that it seems every few years a new generation of workers passes through Packingtown from different heritages, however they are quickly overworked and replaced
  • First it was the Germans, then the Irish, and now the Slovaks
  • She also adds that every family seems to have one or two wage earner die due to accidents or illness from the industry
  • Finally in order to help pay the bills, Ona finds a job as a seamstress and Stanislovas also finds a job after lying about his age
  • Chapter 7
  • The wedding festivities of Ona and Jurgis have put the family over one hundred dollars in debt
  • Many members of the family also are suffering from illnesses from work and the cold weather, yet none of them can take time off to recover
  • The whole family is barely clinging to life as much of the packing industry is hit by waves of illnesses
  • Dede eventually succumbs to deaths call from diseases he received at work
  • Jurgis is very stressed, yet determined to keep his family alive and in a home
  • Chapter 8
  • Marija meets a young musician, Tamoszius, who begins to court her
  • He is very popular among the family as his music livens up the house and also provides opportunities for the family to attend different celebrations
  • The two eventually decide to marry, and they move into the attic of the house
  • Though this is a happy experience, bad times are approaching fast as Marija eventually loses her job when the factory closes
  • Jurgis also loses many hours at his job when shifts must be cut back
  • The family is suffering to make ends meet, and all the workers decide to join Unions to fight for their rights
  • Jurgis begins to recruit many other Lithuanians to the unions, however he comes to realize that many of them have the misguided American dream he once had
  • Chapter 9
  • Jurgis becomes very committed to the Union, he also vows to learn English to help in his efforts
  • He attends night school and has his children help as well, he even decides to become a citizen however this turns out to be a scam as well
  • He is paid two dollars and is instructed by a man to follow his steps to become a citizen and then is taken to a voting booth and follows the mans steps there
  • He ends up being paid to vote for something he has no idea about
  • Jurgis begins to learn more and more about the corruptness of the business, and how the owners of the packing industries are only interested in profits
  • Therefore the workers work for nearly no money in terrible conditions only to pack disgusting meat and mark it as something else
  • Chapter 10
  • Jurgis finally decides to seek out the seller of his house to learn about all the hidden fees
  • He learns that they must pay additional fees for water, insurance, and taxes on top of what they already owe
  • Jurgis is upset, however this is helpful to know so they don't lose the house
  • The winter turns into spring, and the spring into summer
  • The conditions at work become worse with the heat, as flies swarm the meat and the buildings are infernos to work in
  • Marija finally gets her job back, only to lose it two months later
  • Her lose of wages is devastating as Ona is expecting another child
  • Marija eventually finds another job though, this time trimming meat
  • Ona's wages struggle as her supervisor is also the owner of a brothel and prefers to give work to the prostitutes she employs
  • Eventually Ona gives birth to a healthy boy which they name Antanas after Dede, Jurgis' father
  • Jurgis is more committed than ever to work after this, as he wants to provide for his family
  • His work however prevents him from seeing his son often
  • Ona also must work harder, she heads back to work a week after giving birth
  • This however is very harmful to her health
  • Chapter 11
  • Life in Packingtown gets exceedingly tougher for the workers, as the pace of work continues to pick up, yet despite any efforts from the unions, wages are not improving and hours are being cut
  • Marija decides to open a bank account during this time, however this is short lived as soon after she is compelled to withdraw all her money out of fear that she will lose it all
  • She instead decides to sew all her savings into the lining of her clothes, as she feels this will keep it the safest
  • Chapter 12
  • Jurgis gets injured one day and is out of work for months, which is completely frustrating and leaves him in a bitter mood as the lost wages are valuable
  • Stanislovas also suffers a setback when he gets frostbite in his hands, because of this he is often reluctant to go to work causing Jurgis to beat him in order to make him go
  • Meanwhile Jonas disappears, causing more wages to be lost
  • Therefore Nikalojus, Vilimas, and Teta's two sons go out to find jobs as newspaper salesmen to help the family out
  • Chapter 13
  • One day Teta Elzbieta's youngest son dies from eating contaminated beef
  • Teta is crushed, however the rest of the family is slightly relieved, as it is one less mouth to feed, and he was crippled and whiny, and was no use to the family
  • Jurgis has no money to help Teta with, and refuses to help her with the funeral
  • Marija gives what little she can to help
  • After recovering enough from his injury Jurgis seeks out work again, however he has an extremely difficult time finding a job
  • Eventually he finds work at a fertilizer mill, yet this job is terrible, and leaves him covered in chemicals and wreaking of an awful odor
  • The summer brings in more profits for the family, however that does not make life much easier
  • Jurgis' sons who were working selling newspaper have gotten into some dangerous activities on the street causing them to be forced to return to school
  • Teta takes a new job, yet the repetitive nature of her actions causes her health to falter
  • Chapter 14
  • Life becomes ever harder for the family as they struggle to find even terrible work
  • The family is also disgusted with what they know about the meat packing industry yet cannot escape it as they would not survive
  • Jurgis and Ona's relationship begins to suffer due to the life they are forced to live, and Jurgis begins to take up alcohol
  • He nears becoming a full blown alcoholic, yet manages to stop himself despite the torment he faces
  • Antanas suffers greatly from various illnesses yet manages to persevere through his first birthday
  • Meanwhile Ona becomes pregnant again, yet she is very unhealthy this time, and she also suffers from sever depression
  • Chapter 15
  • As winter approaches again work becomes increasingly stressful as work days are increased by many hours
  • Meanwhile Ona fails to return home some nights from work leading to suspicion
  • Jurgis questions her, yet she lies initially before giving in and explaining that she has become her bosses mistress due to threats
  • She adds that he has raped her on occasion, and for the last two months she has been frequenting the brothel to sleep with him because he has threatened to fire her and the rest of the family from their jobs if she doesn't oblige
  • Jurgis is so enraged he storms to the factory and attacks the man, Phil Connor, beating him till he is unconscious and even biting him to the point of drawing blood
  • Eventually he is subdued by a dozen men in the factory and is then brought to the police station
  • Chapter 16
  • Jurgis is taken to jail, he first stays in a cramped cell with dozens of other offenders before his bail and court date is set
  • He is then transported into a small cell of his own, he sits in the cell and contemplates what has occurred
  • He recognizes from the sound of bells outside that it is Christmas Eve causing him to feel terrible as he thinks about missing his family on this holiday
  • Chapter 17
  • While waiting in his cell before the trial Jurgis makes friends with his cellmate, Jack Duane
  • He learns that Jack is from the east and that after a few bad business deals became a safe breaker
  • Jack gives him the address of his mistress and tells him to seek her out if he is ever in need of help as she will know where Jack is
  • Eventually the trial comes, however it is not very helpful to Jurgis as the judge blows off Jurgis' testimony and believes the lies Phil Connor says
  • Jurgis is sentenced to 30 days in jail, a long time for his family who lives day by day
  • While in jail Jurgis is visited by his son Stanislovas who informs him everyone in the family has lost their jobs, due to various reasons and that they now cannot afford rent or food
  • Jurgis is not of much help as all he has is fourteen cents
  • The family is reduced to begging for any income they can make
  • Chapter 18
  • Jurgis' sentence is finally up, however he must stay an extra three days due to a lack of payment for his trial
  • When he is finally out he must walk twenty miles home only to learn that his family has been evicted and is staying at a boarding house
  • After arriving at the house Jurgis can hear Ona's screams from outside, he tries to find her but is stopped and told that she is going into labor, much to early
  • He wants to help and manages to scrounge up just over a dollar for some help from a midwife

  • Jurgis rushes upstairs to find Ona and a priest
  • Ona mutters a few words to Jurgis and then dies
  • Jurgis is crushed and when he comes across Kotrina a few hours later he takes the three dollars she has earned and heads to a bar
  • Chapter 20
  • Teta Elzbieta finally convinces Jurgis to snap out of his drunken depression for the sake of his young son Antanas
  • Jurgis agrees to find work but soon learns that he has been black listed by Connor and cannot receive a job anywhere
  • The remaining family must live off of Teta and Maria’s wages

  • Chapter 21
  • Jurgis desperately searches for another job with no luck

  • He shows deep affection for his young son Antanas and that is what drives him to work hard
  • However one day he returns from work to learn that Antanas has drowned in a freak accident
  • This crushes Jurgis as Antanas was nearly all Jurgis had left in the world to love
  • Chapter 22
  • After seeing Antanas' body Jurgis immediately leaves without a word
  • He begins to walk in no particular direction trying to suppress any emotion and contemplating the nightmare that America has been
  • Eventually he comes across a train and decides to hide in a car, the train takes him far away from Chicago and to the countryside
  • He eventually gets off and bathes in a stream before walking towards a farmhouse nearby
  • At the house he asks a farmer if he can buy food, however he is turned away
  • He walks through the farmers fields and destroys some of the farmers peach trees as a result of overflowing emotion
  • He continues on and finally comes across a nicer farmer who offers him a meal and a place to stay in his barn
  • The man even offers him a job but only can guarantee work through November which annoys Jurgis, as it seems no where can he find work in the winter
  • This surprises the farmer as he believed the cities to always have work in the winter
  • To this Jurgis explains that the belief of an abundance of work always draws more people to the city causing overcrowding and less work in the winter
  • Jurgis ultimately turns down the job offer and continues on as a drifter, he finds meals from place to place, and makes a few dollars here and there doing odd jobs
  • Many farmers are not to sympathetic of him however, and he often has a difficult time finding food causing him to steal
  • During the farming season it is much easier to find work, and Jurgis finds a job that gives him more money than he ever made in Chicago in a short period of time

  • Chapter 23

  • The break sends him to the hospital for two weeks over the Christmas holidays before he is sent to the streets
  • In order to keep warm he attends a religious revival group meeting, however he is disgusted with the people attending and what they preach as he cannot see how they can be optimistic when men like him suffer every day
