Interesting Details

  • Sinclair struggled to find a publisher for his novel, as many viewed it as too appalling and feared it would not sell well. Therefore Sinclair had to come up with the funds himself, and ultimately it turned out to be successful.
  • Sinclair's novel was made into a movie soon after it was published; Sinclair even played a role as the orator of the movie. Currently there are no known versions of the movie, as all have been lost since then.
  • The novel had a profound affect in the meatpacking industry following its publishing. This was due in large part to the public outcry over the working conditions. It helped regulations and standards emerge.
